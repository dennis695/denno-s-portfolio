Pasta Beef and Veggies

A hearty and satisfying meal with beef and fresh vegetables!

Pasta Beef and Veggies Recipe

This hearty recipe is packed with protein, fiber, and delicious flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 200g beef (sliced thinly)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • 1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • 2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • 3. Add the beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Remove the beef and set aside.
  • 4. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion, red bell pepper, and zucchini. Cook for about 5 minutes until the veggies are tender.
  • 5. Add garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
  • 6. Add the cooked beef back into the skillet along with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  • 7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined.
  • 8. Serve the pasta with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired.

Enjoy your Pasta Beef with Veggies!
